## Design Context

### Users
Busy executives who need quick clarity after meetings. They're time-constrained and want to immediately see what matters: their action items, key decisions made, and open questions that need attention. The interface should evoke **confidence and efficiency** — users should trust the AI-extracted insights and move through their review workflow fast.

### Brand Personality
**Professional, precise, minimal.** Assemble is a trustworthy tool that stays out of the way. It surfaces intelligence cleanly without unnecessary decoration. Think Linear or Notion — confident in its simplicity, authoritative in its presentation.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Minimal and spacious. Generous whitespace, content-first hierarchy, subtle color accents.
- **References:** Linear (clean task management), Notion (structured clarity), Apple Notes (simplicity)
- **Anti-references:** Jira (dense/overwhelming), Salesforce (cluttered), overly playful/gamified UIs
- **Theme:** Light mode only
- **Primary color:** Blue (#2563eb) with purple accent (#7c3aed) for AI-related elements
- **Typography:** Inter, clean weight hierarchy (400/500/600/700)

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over decoration** — Every element should serve comprehension. Remove anything that doesn't help the executive scan and act.
2. **Confidence through structure** — Clean hierarchy, consistent spacing, and strong typography make AI-generated content feel trustworthy.
3. **Speed of review** — Design for quick scanning and decisive action. Minimize clicks between seeing an item and approving/assigning it.
4. **Quiet intelligence** — AI features should feel seamlessly integrated, not flashy. The gradient badge and subtle indicators are enough.
5. **Progressive disclosure** — Show the essential information upfront; details on demand through overlays and expandable sections.

### Technical Notes
- Single-file HTML prototype (vanilla CSS/JS, no framework)
- Font: Inter via Google Fonts (400, 500, 600, 700)
- Icons: Inline SVG (Feather-style)
- Desktop-first layout with 260px fixed sidebar
- Accessibility: Best-effort practices (good contrast, focus states, semantic HTML)
- No dark mode
