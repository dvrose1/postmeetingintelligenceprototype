# Assemble - Actions

Post-meeting intelligence prototype that extracts action items, key decisions, and open questions from meeting transcripts.

## Tech Stack
- Single-file HTML prototype (`index.html`) with vanilla CSS/JS
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Icons: Inline SVG (Feather-style)

## Design Context

### Users
Busy executives who need quick clarity after meetings. They're time-constrained and want to immediately see what matters: their action items, key decisions made, and open questions that need attention. The interface should evoke **confidence and efficiency**.

### Brand Personality
**Professional, precise, minimal.** Think Linear or Notion — confident in its simplicity, authoritative in its presentation.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Minimal and spacious. Generous whitespace, content-first hierarchy, subtle color accents.
- **References:** Linear, Notion, Apple Notes
- **Anti-references:** Jira (dense), Salesforce (cluttered), overly playful UIs
- **Theme:** Light mode only
- **Primary color:** Blue (#2563eb) with purple accent (#7c3aed) for AI elements

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over decoration** — Every element should serve comprehension.
2. **Confidence through structure** — Clean hierarchy and strong typography make AI content feel trustworthy.
3. **Speed of review** — Design for quick scanning and decisive action.
4. **Quiet intelligence** — AI features feel seamlessly integrated, not flashy.
5. **Progressive disclosure** — Essential info upfront; details on demand.
